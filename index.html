⚠️ Identified Architectural Risks

Thread Starvation: Flask dev server is single-threaded. Each /stream SSE connection blocks until complete. 2 concurrent users = 1 waits. Fix: Gunicorn -w 2 --threads 2.
No Error Handling: retriever.py and app.py have zero try/except. A Pinecone timeout or Gemini rate-limit crashes the worker.
No Input Validation: user_input can be None, empty, or adversarial. Passes directly to Pinecone search with no length limit.
Dead Config: config.py sets TOP_K=3 but app.py hardcodes top_k=2 in both routes.

Owner: Member 6 · Effort: ~2 hours · Cost: $0

Define 3-5 prompt variants. Configure temperature=[0.0, 0.1, 0.3], top_k=[2, 3, 5]. Benchmark each combo against golden dataset. Track in CSV or MLflow.
Owner: Member 6 + Member 5 · Effort: ~4 hours · Cost: < $0.50
